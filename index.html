Natural Disasters

Earthquake

Earthquake is the sudden shaking of the Earth surface cause by the rapid release of energy in the Earth outer layer,called lithospere.This energy creates seismic waves that travel through the ground

  • Sudden ground shaking
  • Tectonic plate shifts
  • Measured by seismographs

Hurricane

  • Powerful rotating winds
  • Forms over warm oceans
  • Heavy rain and flooding

Landslide

  • Rapid rock/debris flow
  • Triggered by heavy rain
  • Common on steep slopes

Tsunami

  • Large underwater waves
  • Caused by earthquakes
  • High coastal impact
